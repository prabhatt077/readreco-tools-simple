How this salary estimate works

This tool converts your monthly gross salary to an annual figure, then applies a simplified version of India's New Tax Regime slabs to estimate your annual tax and in-hand monthly pay. It does not account for HRA, standard deductions, PF contributions, or other exemptions, so treat it as a rough estimate rather than an exact take-home figure.

Why your actual in-hand pay may differ

Real payslips include deductions like provident fund contributions, professional tax, and sometimes insurance premiums, none of which are modeled here. For an exact number, check your employer's payslip or consult a tax professional.

Frequently asked questions

Is this an exact tax calculation?

No — it's a simplified estimate using illustrative slab rates. Actual tax depends on deductions, exemptions, and your specific tax regime choice.

How do I convert my monthly salary to annual?

Multiply your monthly gross salary by 12 to get your annual gross salary.
